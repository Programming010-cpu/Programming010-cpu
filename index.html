Origin

Born from a late‑night coding sprint in 2025, Zmyrna was crafted as a miniature sandbox to explore tokenomics at nano‑scale. With only 100 units, each transfer writes a footprint onto BNB Smart Chain, reminding us that small experiments lead to big ideas.

Symbolism

The angular Z sigmoid represents a spark—two strokes converging at a point of creation. It nods to Zeno’s paradox: finite supply meeting infinite creativity. Owning a ZMZM is holding a pixel of a larger digital canvas.
