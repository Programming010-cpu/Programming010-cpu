Zmyrna

A minimalist experiment in tokenomics

Origin

Born during a late-night coding sprint, Zmyrna explores tokenomics at nano-scale. With only 100 units minted on BNB Smart Chain, each transfer leaves a permanent footprint.

Symbolism

The angular Z mark symbolizes a spark—two strokes meeting to create something new. It hints at Zeno’s paradox: a finite supply enabling infinite creativity.
